Welcome to the Game Picker!

Do you tremble before the cascade of unplayed games in your Steam library? Well, tremble no more! Your game picking days are over! The Game Picker 9000 is here to do all the picking for you. Simply enter your 17-digit steam ID in the field, hit submit, and in seconds you'll have a freshly selected game to play. If you're not pleased with the selection, press the Reshuffle button for another go-round. But don't press it too many times, or you'll end up just as indecisive as you were before!

Happy gaming!

 

Don't Know Your Steam ID?

  1. Log into the Steam client on your desktop.
  2. In the upper right, where your Steam handle and profile picture are, click on the tiny down arrow to open a dropdown menu.
  3. Select "Account Details" from the dropdown menu.
  4. At the top of the Account Details page you'll see "YourHandle's Account" in large type. Right under that is your Steam ID.
